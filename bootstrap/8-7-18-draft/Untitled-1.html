Our Mission

Improve Baton Rouge and the Capitol Region by partnering with community stakeholders and LSU research expertise to jointly develop and implement Smart Safe & Connected technologies.

Approach

Addressing the region's transportation and crime issues will require a holistic approach, enabled by advanced Smart Safe & Connected (SC&C) technology. We are building a community-based partnership of regional stakeholders and researchers across disciplines. Our efforts include:

  • Engaging relevant community stakeholders,
  • Working with these partners to identify and translate infrastructure issues around transportation and crime, and
  • Utilizing advanced SC&C technologies to address these challenges These ongoing efforts will leverage a planned community-oriented and publicly-available web portal that will enable community stakeholders and researchers to collaborate, share information, and visualize outcomes in real-time.
